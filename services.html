Cybersecurity Services

Risk Assessments & Gap Analysis

Our structured risk assessments identify and prioritize security weaknesses across your IT environment. We evaluate assets, threats, vulnerabilities, and existing controls, then provide actionable recommendations in a report that includes both executive and technical summaries.

Cloud Security & Compliance Audits

We perform comprehensive audits of your cloud environments (AWS, Azure, Microsoft 365) to ensure configurations follow best practices and compliance standards like HIPAA, PCI DSS, and CMMC. Audits include IAM reviews, security baseline checks, logging validation, and remediation roadmaps.

Security Awareness Training

We help employees become your first line of defense. Our training combines phishing simulations, custom e-learning modules, and ongoing micro-trainings. Progress is measured over time to show ROI and reduce human risk.

Phishing Simulations

We conduct simulated phishing attacks to test employee awareness and track high-risk users. Immediate feedback and reporting help reinforce learning and reduce click-through rates over time.

Compliance Guidance

Our team assists with practical, no-nonsense guidance for meeting HIPAA, PCI, and other small-business compliance requirements. We help you understand what’s required, implement solutions, and prepare for audits.

Penetration Testing (Coming Soon)

Soon, we’ll be offering internal and external penetration testing services to simulate real-world attacks. These tests will identify exploitable vulnerabilities and include reports with proof-of-concept findings and step-by-step remediation advice.

Managed IT Services

Remote IT Support

We provide fast, professional remote assistance for day-to-day issues: login problems, email setup, printer errors, software crashes — anything that slows your business down.

Workstation & Device Management

We handle antivirus monitoring, system patching, and performance optimization for Windows and macOS workstations. Our proactive monitoring prevents issues before they cause downtime.

Network Monitoring & Support

From routers and firewalls to remote access VPNs, we ensure your network stays fast, secure, and resilient. We monitor uptime and respond to outages or alerts before they become business problems.

Backup & Continuity Services

Protect your business from data loss or disasters. We design and monitor automated backup solutions with cloud or hybrid storage, and develop continuity plans that help you recover quickly after incidents.

Office 365 & Cloud Support

We manage user accounts, email security, storage, and more — helping your business get the most out of Microsoft 365 and other cloud tools without the headaches.
